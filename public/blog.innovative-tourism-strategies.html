Discover Jamaica's Smart Tourism Vision

Empowering Locals Through Linkages

Enhance Sustainable Practices in Tourism

\n

\n Strengthening tourism's future means putting strong sustainable practices at the center of every decision.\n

\n
\n
\n

Prioritize Coastal Protection

\n

Climate data shows up to 80% of residents live near the coast, making shoreline conservation urgent. Planting mangroves and preserving coral reefs protects communities against erosion.

\n
\n
\n

Support Environmental Assessments

\n

Rigorous reviews ensure responsible growth, protecting habitats like turtle nesting sites while following best practices and national policy.

\n
\n
\n

Foster Community Partnerships

\n

Collaborative no-take zones, like the White River Fish Sanctuary, allow marine life to recover while providing economic stability locally.

\n
\n
\n

Advance Resource Efficiency

\n

Around 40% of earnings stay in-country, supporting energy-saving investments like solar power systems to create a circular economy.
